[w7s.cloud](https://w7s.cloud/) is free to start. You do not need a W7S account, a credit card, or a separate cloud setup to deploy through the hosted service.

GitHub Actions builds your app, the W7S action ships the deploy output, and W7S serves it from a live URL or your custom domain.

## The model

Start free while you build, test, demo, and share your app.

When an app starts getting real traction, pricing is usage-based:

- no subscriptions just to keep an app online;
- no seat fee for every collaborator;
- pay for what the app uses after it has meaningful traffic.

## What usage means

W7S tracks the work needed to serve and operate each deployed app, including deploys, runtime requests, static asset delivery, backend invocations, workflows, queues, logs, and storage-backed features.

The goal is simple: small projects should be easy to launch, and successful projects should scale into usage-based billing instead of a mandatory monthly plan.

## While the service grows

W7S is keeping the hosted experience simple while [w7s.cloud](https://w7s.cloud/) gains traction. If an app grows beyond starter usage, W7S can enable usage billing or coordinate directly with the app owner.

For now, the product promise is the important part:

```text
Start free.
Pay only after your app is a hit.
Pay per use, not by subscription.
```
